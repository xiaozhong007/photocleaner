Sortalyst Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 2026

Last Updated: January 2026

1. Introduction

Sortalyst (referred to as "we," "us," or "our app") takes your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information. By using this app, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information We Collect

Sortalyst is a local photo cleanup tool. We only collect the following necessary information:

  1. Photo Library Access
    • We request access to your photo library to identify screenshots and screen recordings
    • We only access photo metadata (such as creation date, modification date, media type) and identifiers
    • We do NOT read, store, or upload your photo content itself
  2. Identification Result Cache
    • We store identification results locally, including:
      • Photo identifiers
      • Identification results (whether it's a screenshot or screen recording)
      • Identification date and photo modification date
    • This information is only used to improve identification performance and avoid duplicate identification
  3. Cleanup History Statistics
    • We record your cleanup history locally, including:
      • Number of deleted photos
      • Freed storage space (MB)
      • Cleanup time
      • Statistics on screenshots and screen recordings
    • This data is only used to display cleanup results, retained for the last 6 months

2.2 Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Photo content itself
  • Personally identifiable information (name, email, phone number, etc.)
  • Location information
  • Device identifiers
  • Any information that can be used to track your identity

3. How We Use Information

3.1 Purpose of Use

We only use the collected information for the following purposes:

  1. Identification Feature: Use photo metadata and identifiers to identify screenshots and screen recordings
  2. Cleanup Feature: Use identification results to filter photos for cleanup
  3. Statistics Feature: Display cleanup results and history
  4. Performance Optimization: Cache identification results to improve app performance

3.2 Usage Restrictions

We commit to:

  • No Advertising: We will not use your information for advertising or tracking
  • No Marketing: We will not use your information for marketing purposes
  • No Other Purposes: We will not use your information for any purpose not stated in this policy

4. Information Storage

4.1 Storage Location

All information is stored locally on your device using iOS's UserDefaults storage mechanism.

4.2 Stored Content

We only store:

  • Photo identifiers (not photo content)
  • Identification results (boolean values)
  • Statistics (numbers)

We do NOT store:

  • Photo content itself
  • Complete photo data
  • Any personally identifiable information

4.3 Data Retention

  • Identification Result Cache: Retained permanently until you delete the app or clear the cache
  • Cleanup History: Only the last 6 months of data is retained, older data is automatically deleted

5. Information Sharing

5.1 No Sharing Principle

We do NOT share your information with any third parties, including:

  • Not shared with other companies or organizations
  • Not shared with advertisers
  • Not shared with data analytics services
  • Not shared with any other apps or services

5.2 Local Processing

All data processing is done locally on your device:

  • All identification operations are performed locally on your device
  • All data is stored locally on your device
  • No data is uploaded to any server
  • No network transmission occurs

6. Data Security

6.1 Security Measures

  • All data is stored locally on your device, protected by iOS security mechanisms
  • We only store identifiers and statistics, not sensitive content
  • Data is stored in JSON-encoded format

6.2 Data Deletion

You can at any time:

  • Revoke photo library access permission in iOS Settings
  • Delete the app to clear all local data
  • Clear app data (through iOS Settings)

7. Your Rights

7.1 Access and Control

You have the right to:

  1. Access Permissions: View and manage photo library access permissions in iOS Settings at any time
  2. Revoke Permissions: Revoke photo library access permission at any time
  3. Delete Data: Clear all locally stored data by deleting the app
  4. Understand Information: Understand how we collect and use information through this Privacy Policy

7.2 Data Control

  • You can stop using this app at any time
  • You can revoke permissions at any time, and the app will stop accessing your photo library
  • After deleting the app, all local data will be cleared

8. Children's Privacy

This app is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you are a parent or guardian and discover that your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

9. Privacy Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. For significant changes, we will notify you through the app or by other means. Continued use of this app indicates your acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.
