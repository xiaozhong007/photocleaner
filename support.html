About PhotoCleaner

PhotoCleaner is a smart photo cleanup tool that helps you quickly identify and remove screenshots and screen recordings from your iPhone, freeing up valuable storage space.

Key Features

  • Smart Identification: Automatically identifies screenshots and screen recordings using advanced AI technology
  • One-Click Cleanup: Batch select and delete unwanted photos with just one tap
  • Space Statistics: See exactly how much storage space you can free up
  • Privacy First: All processing is done locally on your device
  • Completely Free: No in-app purchases, no subscriptions, no advertisements

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What does PhotoCleaner do?
A: PhotoCleaner automatically identifies and helps you remove screenshots and screen recordings from your photo library, freeing up storage space on your iPhone.
Q: Will PhotoCleaner delete my important photos?
A: No. PhotoCleaner only identifies screenshots and screen recordings. You can preview all photos before deleting, and deleted photos will be moved to the "Recently Deleted" album where they can be recovered within 30 days.
Q: Does PhotoCleaner require internet connection?
A: No. PhotoCleaner works completely offline. All processing is done locally on your device, and no data is uploaded to any server.
Q: Does PhotoCleaner collect or share my data?
A: No. PhotoCleaner does not collect, store, or share any of your personal data. All processing is done locally on your device, and your photos never leave your device.
Q: Is PhotoCleaner free to use?
A: Yes. PhotoCleaner is completely free to use. There are no in-app purchases, no subscriptions, and no advertisements.
Q: How accurate is the identification?
A: PhotoCleaner uses advanced AI technology to identify screenshots and screen recordings with high accuracy. However, we recommend reviewing selected photos before deleting to ensure you don't remove anything important.
Q: Can I recover deleted photos?
A: Yes. When you delete photos using PhotoCleaner, they are moved to the "Recently Deleted" album in the Photos app, where they can be recovered within 30 days.
Q: What iOS version is required?
A: PhotoCleaner requires iOS 15.0 or later. It works on both iPhone and iPad.
Q: Why does PhotoCleaner need photo library access?
A: PhotoCleaner needs access to your photo library to identify screenshots and screen recordings. We only access photo metadata (such as creation date, modification date, media type) and identifiers. We do NOT read, store, or upload your photo content itself.
Q: How do I revoke photo library access?
A: You can revoke photo library access at any time by going to Settings > Privacy & Security > Photos > PhotoCleaner, and selecting "None" or "Never".

How to Use

Getting Started

  1. Open PhotoCleaner on your iPhone
  2. Grant photo library access when prompted
  3. Wait for the app to scan your photos (this may take a few minutes for large libraries)
  4. Review the cleanup suggestions on the home screen
  5. Tap on a suggestion to view and select photos for deletion
  6. Review selected photos and tap "Delete" to remove them

Tips for Best Results

  • Review selected photos before deleting to ensure you don't remove anything important
  • Use the time filter options to focus on older photos
  • Check the cleanup history to track your progress
  • Deleted photos can be recovered from "Recently Deleted" album within 30 days
